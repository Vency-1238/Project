About Punnerva

Our Mission

Punnerva is dedicated to fostering a community of wellness and mindfulness at PDEU. We aim to introduce students to the transformative power of yoga, helping them build physical strength, enhance mental clarity, and find inner peace amidst the rigors of academic life.

Our Story

Founded in [Year], Punnerva started as a small group of students passionate about yoga. Today, we have grown into a vibrant club that hosts regular classes, special workshops, and community events, welcoming everyone from absolute beginners to experienced yogis.
